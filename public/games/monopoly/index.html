ESTATES PRO RULEBOOK

Objective

Become the wealthiest player through buying, renting, and selling properties while bankrupting your opponents.

Property Trading & Buildings

When you land on an unowned property, you can buy it for the listed price. Once owned, other players who land on it must pay you rent.

  • Monopoly sets: If you own all properties of the same color, the base rent is doubled.
  • Houses & Hotels: After achieving a monopoly, you can build up to 4 houses, and eventually a hotel. Buildings massively increase rent! You can only build on your turn and before throwing the dice.

Chance & Community Chest

Landing on these spaces means drawing a card. Cards might give you tax refunds, make you pay unexpected fees, or fast-travel you across the board.

Doubles & Jail (IN JAIL)

If you roll doubles (e.g. two 3s), you take another turn. However, if you roll doubles 3 times in a row, you are caught 'speeding' and sent directly to Jail without passing GO!

While in jail, you cannot move, but you can still collect rent from properties you own.

Mortgages

If you are low on cash, click on your owned property to Mortgage it. You will receive 50% of the property's cost back. However, mortgaged properties cannot collect rent until they are unmortgaged (which costs the mortgage amount + 10% interest).

Bankruptcy

If you owe more money than you have and cannot raise funds by mortgaging or selling houses, you are officially bankrupt and eliminated from the game!
